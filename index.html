About LV8 Event

Welcome to the LV8 Event, a one-of-a-kind educational experience designed to empower students and educators in the digital world.

At LV8, we believe in the power of digital education and innovation. Our event brings together students, teachers, and technology experts for a day filled with interactive activities, workshops, and challenges.

By participating in the LV8 Event, students have the opportunity to enhance their digital skills, solve real-world problems, and learn about the latest technology trends. They can network with industry partners, gain insights from inspiring speakers, and explore career opportunities in the digital realm.

Our goal is to foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and lifelong learning. We want to empower the next generation of digital leaders and equip them with the skills they need to thrive in an increasingly digital world.

Come join us at the LV8 Event and unlock your potential in the digital space!

What is LV8?

LV8 is a free mobile app designed to provide young people with digital training opportunities. It utilizes gamification techniques, turning learning into an engaging game-like experience. It aims to help individuals who are not studying or working to develop digital skills that can enhance their employability and future prospects.

The app covers various digital competencies, including the use of Google applications, graphic design, SEO, social media strategy, CMS, coding, and more. LV8 consists of eight levels, and upon completion of each level, users receive digital certifications called Open Badges. These certifications are recognized by the European community and can be showcased in resumes.

LV8 also offers self-assessment tests and access to targeted courses and opportunities for further skill development.
